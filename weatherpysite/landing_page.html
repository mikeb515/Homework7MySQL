Why are we here?

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate the various types of weather that can be effected by the lattitude of a given postion on the Earth. Using the location of over 600 cities, the four graphs on this site compare the humidity, wind speed, average maximum temperature and relative cloudiness to those cities' inherent latitude.

What is Latitude?

Before we get into the graphs, let's discuss what latitude is. Lattitude is generally defined as the distance of given position on the surface of the Earth relative to the equator and expressed in degrees which increase with distance.
